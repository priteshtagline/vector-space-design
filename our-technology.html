Our technology

Our technology powers research groups in space biosciences along with data vendors, and institutions by generating on-demand NLP/NLU correlation matrix datasets. We are particularly interested in how we can get machines to trade information or exchange and transact data in a way that minimizes a selected loss function. Our objective is to enable any group analyzing data to save time by testing a hypothesis or running experiments with higher throughput. This will increase the speed of innovation, novel scientific breakthroughs, and discoveries.

REAL-TIME UPDATES AT SCALEL

Access to near real-time (NRT) datasets that update as frequently as once per minute (1440 API calls per day), allowing for near real-time correlation scores and insights that can be generated in isolation, or as an augmentation to any external or internal dataset.

APPLICATIONS

Applications exist across any area of research rooted in human language, and new ways of leveraging proprietary technology is a continuous pursuit with collaborators, partners and clients. Space biosciences is a focus because of subject matter expertise pedigree and the importance of discovery in this area to humanity. We strive to better understand measures to protect and repair human DNA and relationships between diseases, genes, proteins, drug compounds and micronutrients - in many instances research is conducted across multiple areas to unlock discovery.

Security

Datasets represent a large attack surface in overall data security associated to AI and ML pipelines. Knowing the origin of data sources, calculations, algorithms, timestamps along with any other changes remain critical for today’s scientific data engineering pipelines.

Data Provenance, governance, lineage

Data provenance, governance, and lineage are indispensable to research: knowing where your data comes from and how reliable it is are particularly important in Bioscience, so our Data Provenance Pipeline (DPP) hash controls data lineage.

VXV Utility

VXV token wallet-enabled API key allows any company to subscribe to our API services in order to stream NLP/NLU context-controlled datasets on-demand. Tiered subscription levels, each requiring a different amount of VXV, allow for specialized services and give advanced users options on select, high-value datasets.

Request a Dataset with Custom Features

While our NLP/NLU datasets are endlessly customizable and can be leveraged by any industry, we also provide access to many high quality data sources out-of-the-box.
